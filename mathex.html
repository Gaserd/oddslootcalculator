Mathematical expectation

Added info and calculate mathematical expectation

To calculate the mathematical expectation, enter the coefficients that you see from your bookmaker and set the winning percentages for the team. After that, the calculator will calculate the odds that the bookmaker should have based on your winning percentages. It also calculates the mathematical expectation of your bet (as an example, take that you bet $100). The mathematical expectation means the following - how much money you will get if you bet on this bet every time, but if your mathematical expectation is marked with a plus sign, it does not mean that your bet will win.
