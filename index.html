Oddsloot Calculator

Money for betts – $

This project works very simply.
It parses data from oddsloot.com and considers where you would put the most profitable.
He thinks based on the fact that the main bet is the first team and it will be $10, then calculates how much you need to put on the 2 bet so that you are in the black.
It highlights the best outcomes in gold, where you will be in the black if you win any of the teams.
Under each bet written by the bookmaker where it is necessary to put a bet.
